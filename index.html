About Nicholas Caldwell (fictional)

Nick grew up quickly, homesteading offgrid with his 10 brothers and sisters far from civilization in the Rocky Mountains. There was always work to be done and younger siblings to manage. When he came down from the mountains as an adult, this work ethic carried him through many successes.

Education

While lacking a completed degree, Nick has accumulated over 115 credit hours from various colleges and universities. This has been supplemented with various IT industry certifications. As a side quest, Nick took some time to get a commercial pilot's license which has, unfortunately, been unused for some time.

Experience

As a roofer in Connecticut and Electrician in Florida for brief periods, Nick gained valuable experience that contributed to his current work as a property manager since last year. He is simultaneously managing a medical office, which he has done for the past 20 years.

Interests

A father of six and grandfather to four, Nick devotes his best efforts to fullfilling those roles. This means his interests are as varied as his family members: archery, sword fighting, programming, cooking, babysitting, gaming, and general self-improvement.
